Who We Are

It started with a toilet leak in our apartment building, which turned into a drain leak, then a backup into the tub all in the space of a week. Our landlord refused to repair and the services of other “professionals” was subpar. After successfully repairing a busted water heater, laying a new floor, doing a complete re-pipe AND a sewage change out, we put our heads together to form PLUMBERS UNITE. Our headquarters is in our fixer upper apartment building in downtown Bronx, where we can work live and build our community. We have many different professional backgrounds and different skills, but we all enjoy one thing, working together to get the job done!! Founded in the late 2022's we are in the business to stop it, fix it, and maintain it so that you never have to worry. Our goal is to provide exceptional service, with quality work at affordable pricing. If we have gone through it, then we know you have too.
We are certified in all divisions and are expanding. Check out our testimonials for more information, and get a quote today!!!!
